Greens Food Suppliers
Greens Food Suppliers

Location: Media, PA

Greens is a family owned market with locations in Swarthmore (since 1991) and Media, PA (since 2011) specializing in fresh, seasonal lunch and dinner options, craft beer, natural wine and local grass fed cheeses. We have a cafe at each location where you can enjoy one of our amazing salads, sandwiches, burgers and an assortment of vegan options. Stay for dinner and pick from a huge selection of craft beer and uniquely curated natural wine. We've recently been recognized by both local and national press as having one of best natural wine selections in Philadelphia and beyond.
